who we are

half past nine is a 4-piece band formed in 2018 from Sydney, Nova Scotia.
First know as Jester, half past nine changed their band name in 2023, as we felt it better suits our group.
First friends then bandmates, we came together bonding over our wide variety in music tastes.
From indie-pop to thrash-metal, our tastes vary greatly, but that's what we think helps develop our sound.
Our sound can change song to song, sounds ranging from indie-rock to more mellow bedroom-pop.

Ethan is one of the guitarists and vocalists in half past nine, and is the busiest as well, whether he's writing a new riff or track to fit into a demo, Ethan always has something new to show us.

Boyd is the other guitarist and a vocalist for half past nine, can always be found playing the same Her's songs on guitar or trying to get the right chorus sound.

Nolan is the bass guitarist and a vocalist for half past nine, and is one of the only guys who has any music theory knowledge. He comes up with some of the most musically wacky sounds in our demos and live recordings, bringing life to our live shows.

Cameron is the drummer and a vocalist for half past nine, and can come up with drum tracks on a dime. Having lots of experience in metal drumming, Cam knows when to slow things down or speed things up.
